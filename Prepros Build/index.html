New Workshops

Electronics & Robotics

In this workshop, the students will learn basic concepts of electronics and tinker with LED's, switches, motors, sensors and Arduino. They will learn how to draw circuits on paper and make them work using LED's and Battery. Finally, they will learn how to make their own obstacle avoiding autonomous robot using Arduino.

Computer Programming

The students will learn how to program their own interactive stories, games and animation while developing logical thinking ability using Scratch programming language. They will also learn basics of programming in python and make small interactive projects.

About Us

TechWeekends is an initiative to inspire young school and college students about Electronics, Robotics and Computer Programming.

Students will unleash their creativity and learn about problem solving and logical thinking while tinkering during the workshops and have a lot of fun doing it!
